Breaking Bad Fans Are Livid With Bryan Cranston And Aaron Paul Over Their Mezcal Project Heres

 Posted on September 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  402 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Breaking Bad fans are livid with Bryan Cranston and Aaron Paul over their new project.

After weeks of teasing what fans believed was the Breaking Bad sequel movie, Cranston and Paul revealed on Tuesday what they have been working on for months. News flash it is not the movie sequel, nor does it have anything to do with this hit AMC show.

The Breaking Bad costars are teaming up to release a Mezcal called Dos Hombres. Paul made the announcement, as well as explained how the project came about via Instagram. He began explaining the two were having dinner discussing how they could work together again, knowing acting together might be out the window for a while.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BlV7POfg7x0/

“The younger one looked at his drink and said, you know what we should do? We should do a really special Mezcal. The older one said, you mean the liquor with a worm at the bottom? Nah, that was just some bullshit gimmick, I mean real, artesanal Mezcal made by hand in Mexico,” he shared in his message explained how the project came about.

Paul went on to say he and Cranston even traveled to Oaxaca to find a version “so damn good even people who don’t think they like Mezcal will love it.”

“We named it Dos Hombres – two guys on a quest. It’s been a long and crazy journey, and we couldn’t be happier to share this with you and the rest of the world,” he shared before ending the message explaining where people could find out more about Dos Hombres.

Cranston and Paul are thrilled with their new venture, but Breaking Bad fans are fuming mad. They feel cheated and disappointed after weeks of tricks leading them to believe the men were reuniting Walter and Jesse.
