Rihanna and ASAP Rocky have been in her native Barbados, celebrating the holidays together and just having the time of their lives! That being said, one insider report shared how the songstress’ loved ones felt about having her new boyfriend by their side during Christmas!

While the two stars are yet to officially confirm their relationship, it’s pretty much a sure thing at this point since they were pictured packing the PDA on Christmas Eve while in Barbados.

They were seen holding hands while on a walk together and then they went on to celebrate the holidays in each other’s company, alongside Rihanna’s family members!

While that was a big step in their new romance, one source tells HollywoodLie that Rihanna’s loved ones had nothing against having ASAP Rocky in their home.

‘The trip is going so well — it could not be going any better. He fits in perfectly with her family and friends. It is a big relief for Rihanna because her family tends to be very protective of her. They are all very genuine and humble people, so the fact that Rocky was not born with a silver spoon in his mouth also plays in his favor. Plus, his intentions with her are really sincere, and everybody can see just how much he cares about her.’

‘That is all that really matters to Rihanna’s family. Obviously, it is still too soon to say where this relationship will go, but it is exciting for everyone that she has taken the step to bring him home for the holidays.’

While it seems like Rihanna may have not brought anyone else home so early on in their relationship, when it comes to ASAP Rocky, things are obviously different since they have been friends for many years!
