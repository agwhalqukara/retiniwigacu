Nothing beats the excitement for a player to return his old team. Going by the lines, Nick Foles homecoming to his previous NFL team is a delight reunification.

Born as Nicholas Edward Foles, the athlete wears number nine jersey for the NFL team, Philadelphia Eagles. Nick, age 30 started his career with the Eagles in 2012 with his much more exciting return to the team again in 2017.

Nick Foles Bio & Family

The 30-year-old was born on 20 January 1989 in Austin, Texas as Nicholas Edward Foles. His parents, Larry and Melissa Foles raised him and his brother, Marcus. Nick’s dad runs a restaurant after dropping out his high school.

Nick went to Westlake High School to complete graduation in 2007 followed by communication degree from the University of Arizona. Nick stands at the height of 6 feet 6 inches and weighs around 110 kg.

Career & Net Worth

Nick has a net worth of $13 million solely from his professional NFL career of over eight years. On 12 May 2012, the Philadelphia Eagles penned him a 4-year contract until 2016. In the very season, Nick completed 22 passes out of 32 attempts for 219 yards, one touchdown, and one interception.

With three seasons with Eagles, Nick went on a draft to St. Louis Rams on 10 March 2015. The Rams offered him a two-year contract worth $24.5 million on 7 August 2015. However, the Kansas City Chiefs signed him for a year deal of $1.75 million on 3 August 2016. The contact stated that he would be paid between $6.75 million and $16 million only from his performance. Nevertheless, he became the free agent after his second-year contract cancellation from Chiefs on 9 March 2017.

The quarterback returned to his former club, Eagles for a two-year agreement on 13 March 2017. According to Eagles contract, Nick makes an annual salary of atleast $4 million. The very next season, the Chiefs revised Nick’s contract of extra $2 million bonus on 20 April 2018.

Nick’s Wife Connection To NFL

Nick shares an abundant love and trust with his wife, Tori Moore. His partner is the sister of former American tight end, Evan Moore. The beautiful pair first met in school and after college agreed to get married in April 2014.

The footballer and his caring wife were the students of the University of Arizona. During the time, the pair had a mutual admiration into sports where Nick played football and Tori was involved in playing volleyball. Together the couple shares a daughter named Lily James Foles, born on 16 June 2017.

Nick is a complete family guy and loves to spend most of his free time with his partner, Tori and their bundle of joy.
