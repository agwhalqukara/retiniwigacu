American football quarterback Jameis Winston plays for NFL team, Tampa Bay Buccaneers. The number three man of Buccaneers is the 2013 recipient of Heisman Trophy, Walter Camp Award, and Manning Award.

Jameis Winston Bio & Family

The player was born as Jameis Lanaed Winston on 6 January 1994 in Bessemer, Alabama to parents, Antonor and Loretta Winston. He went to Hueytown High School and then Florida State University on 3 February 2012. Jameis played 2012, 2013 and 2014 season for his college. One of his essential playing highlights was passing for 235 yards with two touchdowns in the 2013 season.

Jameis stands at the height of 6 feet 4 inches (1.93 m) tall and weighs around 105 kg.

Career & Net Worth

The 25-year-old has a net worth of $12 million from a professional NFL career. After a successful playing record, he got the fortune to be in the 2015 NFL with Tampa Bay Buccaneers. He and Buccaneers signed a four-year contract worth $23.35 million including a signing bonus of $16.7 million on 1 May 2015.

It’s been over four years that he is with Buccaneers. One of his recent record playings is finishing 194 passing yards, one touchdown, and three interceptions, 2 of which were returned for touchdowns in the 2019 season.

He and his wife-to-be Breon Allen got engaged in Spring 2017 on the same day where Prince Harry and Meghan Markle exchanged their engagement rings.
