What is the point of Naked and Afraid?

What is primitive survival rating?

PSR, which stands for Primitive Survival Rating, is the criterion used by specialists to assess the survival skills of individuals. PSR is calculated using three major elements of performance: primitive skills, mental strength, and experience.
