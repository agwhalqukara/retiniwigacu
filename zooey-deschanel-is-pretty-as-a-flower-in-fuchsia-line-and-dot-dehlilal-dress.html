Zooey Deschanel Is Pretty As A Flower In Fuchsia Line And Dot Dehlilal Dress

 Posted on July 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  373 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Zooey Deschanel shared several photos of herself on her official Twitter account where the New Girl star was seen leaping in the air while jumping on a bed as she held a bright-yellow pillow. Zooey had asked if jumping on the bed counts as a cardio workout but what fans couldn’t stop talking about was the gorgeous, fuchsia-colored dress she wore. The official name of the dress is the Dehlilal dress from Line and Dot.  It is a mini that comes slightly down to her mid-thigh and features short, puffed sleeves. It also features a v-neck dress with a cinched waist-band that accentuated Zooey’s figure. The dress is a good choice for those who are looking for a feminine and colorful dress to wear and retails for approximately $92.

Zooey received tons of praise for the dress and the official Line and Dot Instagram account shared the photos of Zooey wearing the dress on their Instagram page. The colors on the photo are very artistic as Zooey is jumping on the bed underneath a beautiful, crystal chandelier. The bed frame features deeply carved, wood panels with two wood nightstands featuring additional carved details. Two lamps appear on each side and they appear to be brass — a fragrant candle can be seen on one of the tables.

The bed covering is silver with white, detailed etchings that appear to be printed on the fabric rather than embroidered. The most noticeable color in the photo is Zooey’s fuchsia dress followed by the yellow pillows.
