Married At First Sight star Ella Ding has come out swinging against her former ‘husband’ Mitchell Eynaud after their romance came crashing down post-reunion.

On Monday, the beautician, 28, described her ex as an ‘enigma’ who ‘doesn’t believe in monogamous relationships’, before saying he was the ‘worst guy I’ve ever dated’.

Speaking on The Handbags podcast, she scathed: ‘He’s the coldest person I’ve ever met.

‘At the wedding they literally just aired us kissing. We were very awkward. In terms of affection, he had no idea.’

She went on to discuss his views on relationships, claiming: ‘He doesn’t believe in monogamous relationships. Mitchell is an enigma.

‘I’ve dated a lot of men, and I am good at reading people. I have never met anyone like Mitchell. He is wired so differently.

Ella went on to say that while she had every intention of keeping the pair’s romance going after the reunion.

However, it all came crashing down when she was pictured enjoying a day at the beach with Brent Vitiello on Valentine’s Day.

‘As soon as they came out, he showed a really ugly side,’ she added, claiming that their pal Domenica Calarco was also present.

‘It was all about the way that he looked. He knew I was at the beach.’
