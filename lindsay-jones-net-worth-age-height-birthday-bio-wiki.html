Lindsay Jones Biography

Lindsay Jones is one of the most popular and richest YouTube Star who was born on September 6, 1989 in Dallas, TX, Texas, United States. She is a member of the Rooster Teeth team along with Barbara Dunkelman.

In April 2012, she was added to the cast of three-time Podcast Award winner Internet Box.

Jones married her long-time boyfriend Michael Jones on May 9, 2014. During Rooster Teeth’s 2016 Extra Life livestream, Jones and Michael announced that they were expecting a daughter, due in June 2017. Iris Elise Jones was born on May 24, 2017. At RTX 2018, Jones announced that she was expecting a second child due in February 2019. Luna Claire Jones was born February 18, 2019.

She married Michael Jones in 2014, a fellow Achievement Hunter and Rooster Teeth employee. She and Michael have daughters named Iris and Luna.

She created the idea of Let’s Play Minecraft episodes “Creeper Soccer” and “Buckingham Palace.”

Lindsay Elise Jones (née Tuggey, born September 6, 1989) is an American actress, voice actress, director, gamer and host. She is best known for her work at Rooster Teeth, with roles such as Ruby Rose in RWBY and Kimball in Red vs. Blue. She was also a director, writer and voice actress on X-Ray and Vav, a cartoon series by Rooster Teeth. Additionally, Jones was the primary manager of Rooster Teeth’s video gaming website Achievement Hunter, and has appeared in many other Rooster Teeth productions such as Ten Little Roosters and On the Spot.
