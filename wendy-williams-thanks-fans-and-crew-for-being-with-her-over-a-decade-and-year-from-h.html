Wendy Williams Thanks Fans And Crew For Being With Her Over A Decade And Year From H***

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  307 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Wendy Williams found that the tables have turned on her this year when she went from talking about hot topics to being one. The talk show recently aired the last episode of the year where she thanked her fans and crew for being with her over a decade.

This year alone, Wendy went from being a married mom to divorcing her husband who had a baby with his mistress, lived in a sober house, and was diagnosed with Lymphedema.

Earlier this year, there was barely a week that Wendy or her soon-to-be ex-husband didn’t make headlines.

Now, the soon-to-be-single woman has had a hot girl summer where she made new celebrity friends and went on multiple dates with men.

She has a new home, new pets, and a new lease on life.

During today’s episode, she told her studio audience: ‘It’s the closing of a decade! Do you realize we’ve been together, everybody, for a decade plus. We’ve seen everything! I love you all, you are the voice inside my head. I love my people, I love this show.’

She added that she doesn’t care about the haters who criticize her for some of the things she talks about.

‘And, if you don’t like that I talk about my personal life or whatever, then change the channel. I don’t care. It’s been a year from hell and now I’m back!’

This comes after Stevie J revealed while he was on the show that she wants a divorce for Christmas.

The latest reports indicate that Kevin Hunter Sr. is giving Williams a hard time.
